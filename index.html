Transport

  • The only public transport here are the busses. There are numerous busses servicing the area with numerous bus stops.

  • However, if busses aren't for you, taxis and ride share businesses also operate in the area.

Cool Facts

  • Has a population of 8,271
  • Encompasses an area of 7.1km2
  • It's postcode is 4868

Housing Stats

  • A median property price of $375,000
  • An average rent price at $410 per week

Proximity

  • 11km from the Cairns CBD
  • 16km from the Cairns Airport
  • 11km from the Cairns Marlin Marina, and is also close to boat ramps for trailer boats to be able to explore the Great Barrier Reef

Shopping

There are many options for shopping in the area:

  • Mount Sheridan Plaza is the biggest shopping centre on this side of Cairns:
    • Here you will find Coles, Woolworths, Kmart and other specialty stores
    • There is also a dentist, doctor, X-ray centre, bank and gym
  • Bentley Village:
    • This is a collection of specialty stores, including a butcher, newsagent, nightowl and chemmist
    • Is where the department of transport is located
  • A corner store
  • Takeaway shops and fast food outlets are situated throughout the area, including Red Rooster and Hungry Jacks



Fretwell Park

  • Is the home of the South Cairns CUtters AFL team
  • Has an indoor stadium for court sports

    • A great restaurant with a kids playground, bar and pokies

Education

  • A primary Catholic school
  • A prep to grade 12 public school:
    • Well set out school with lots of gardens and grass spaces for running around
    • Primary school has multiple playgrounds for the children
    • The high school caters for various fields of study, including, mathematics, English, hospitality, technology, INTAD, sports and many more


Other Key Points Of Interest


Parklands

There are many playgrounds and open grassy parks in Mount Sheridan. Lots of kids use the undercover skate park and basketball court while others run around on the open spaces, fly kites and have fun on the playgrounds. The parks around here are great for spending time with family and friends in the outdoors. There is also some exercise equipment for the public to use.






Sports

There are a variety of sports on offer in the Mount Sheridan area. These include, but are not limited to: AFL, cricket, gymnastics, boxing, soccer, baseball and softball. There are gyms in the area that cater for serious gym junkies all the way through to beginners.


Services

Mount Sheridan offers essential services to the public. Some of these include:

  • Service station
  • Department of Transport
  • Hardware store
  • Fire station


Kindergartens and Day Care Centres

This neighbourhood is proud to boast numerous kindergarten and day care centres. These are located throughout the suburb.

The kindergartens provide teaching for young children just before they are old enough to attend school, whereas the day care centres look after young children whhile their parents are working.
